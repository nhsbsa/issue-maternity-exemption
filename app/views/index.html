Issue maternity exemption certificates Version 1.0

This service allows midwives, practice nurses or health visitors to issue maternity exemption certificates on behalf of new or expectant mothers.

Sprint 68

Using NHS.UK design system for issuing maternity exemption certificates

Hypothesis A

We believe that using the NHS.UK design system for issuing maternity exemption certificates will be a useful feature for every midwife, practice nurse or health visitor who is applying on behalf of a expectant mother as it will reassure them that the service is a part of the wider group of NHS services. This will help midwives, practice nurses or health visitors accomplish their needs more efficiently due to the fact we are now being more consistant.

The NHS Identity is one of the most cherished and recognised brands in the world. When applied correctly and consistently, it evokes exceptionally high levels of trust and reassurance.

By using NHS Business Services Authority branding for each patient facing service, we are unknowingly diluting the power of the NHS brand.

In short, by being more consistant with NHS.UK, usability and learnability will improve because we will be using similar elements which look and function in similar way.

What we changed:

  • We replicated the current maternity exemption service into the NHS.UK design system.

What we plan to test:

  • Are users still able to successfully issue maternity exemption certificates on behalf of expectant mothers?
  • Does usability improve because we are now being more consistant with the wider NHS?
  • As a bonus of adopting the new design system, are we now more accessible than we were previously?

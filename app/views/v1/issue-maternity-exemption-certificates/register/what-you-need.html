What you'll need

To create an account you must be a midwife, practice nurse or health visitor registered with the NMC.

To help us verify your professional identity, we'll ask for your:

  • last name
  • date of birth
  • NMC PIN
  • NHSmail address

Registration takes less than 5 minutes.

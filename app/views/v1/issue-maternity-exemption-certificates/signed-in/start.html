Issue maternity exemption certificates

This service allows midwives, practice nurses or health visitors to issue maternity exemption certificates on behalf of new or expectant mothers.

A maternity exemption certificate entitles the mother to free prescriptions:

  • while pregnant
  • 12 months after giving birth

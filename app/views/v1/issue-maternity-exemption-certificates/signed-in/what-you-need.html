What you'll need

You must be a midwife, practice nurse or health visitor registered with an NMC.

To issue maternity exemption certificates on behalf of new or expectant mothers, you'll need the mother's:

  • date of birth
  • NHS number
  • due or birth date of their child
  • email or postal address
